How I Work

SELECTIVE BY DESIGN.

At any given time I work with a small number of clients — typically up to three partnerships running simultaneously, each one different, each one getting my full attention and commitment. I'm selective by design, not by accident. The work is better for it, and so are the relationships.

Current long-term partnership

My current long-term partnership is with Vault X — a fast-scaling consumer brand in Poole whose values mirror my own: be better, be different, be kind. It's exactly the kind of relationship I look for.

What I'm drawn to

Beyond my existing commitments, I'm always open to conversations with the right people. I'm particularly interested in working with creative education organisations — universities, academies, schools doing something genuinely different — and purpose-driven organisations in the third sector or social enterprise space. That said, the best partnerships are often the ones you don't see coming. If you think there's something worth exploring, I'd rather have the conversation than not.

The conversation costs nothing. And you might be surprised where it leads.
