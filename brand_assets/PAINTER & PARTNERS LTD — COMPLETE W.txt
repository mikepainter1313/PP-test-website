SECTION 1 — BRAND GUIDELINES
Logo
The logo consists of a circular wave icon and the wordmark "Painter & Partners Ltd". The wave icon graduates from deep navy through mid ocean blue to bright cyan with white foam highlights. The wordmark uses deep navy for "PAINTER" and bright cyan for "& PARTNERS LTD". The logo should always appear on white or very light backgrounds. Do not place the logo on dark or heavily saturated backgrounds.
Colour Palette
Primary background: #FFFFFF — clean white, used for all main page backgrounds
Secondary background: #F0F7FA — very pale ice blue, used for alternating sections and cards
Deep navy (logo dark): #1B3A5C — used for primary headings, nav, and strong text elements
Ocean blue (logo mid): #2E86AB — used for subheadings, links, and secondary UI elements
Cyan (logo bright): #00B4D8 — used for highlighted text, active states, borders and interactive elements
Green flash accent: #2ECC71 — used sparingly and only for special moments — the green flash motif, key callouts, moments of significance
Sun yellow accent: #F5C518 — used sparingly for warmth and highlights — the sun and horizon motif only
Body text: #2C3E50 — dark charcoal, used for all paragraph text
Muted text: #7F9BA6 — soft blue-grey, used for supporting text, labels and captions
Dividers / borders: #D6E8EF — pale blue-grey, used for section borders and card outlines
Typography
All type is sans-serif throughout — clean, modern, geometric. No serif fonts anywhere on the site. Choose a distinctive, characterful sans-serif that feels fresh and considered rather than defaulting to mainstream choices. Avoid Inter, Roboto and Arial.
Headings use capitalisation prominently — section headings and key statements in uppercase or title case for impact and clarity. Body text in sentence case.
Type hierarchy:

H1 (hero headline): large, bold, uppercase, commanding
H2 (section heading): medium-large, uppercase or title case, strong weight
H3 (card/subsection heading): medium, title case, medium weight
Body: light weight, generous line height (1.8), easy to read
Labels / eyebrows: small, uppercase, wide letter spacing, used above headings to introduce sections
Buttons: uppercase, wide letter spacing, medium weight

Tone and Feel
Light, open and airy. White space is generous. The ocean and surf references feel natural and woven in — never forced or gimmicky. Think premium coastal lifestyle brand rather than surf shop. Clean lines, considered typography, restrained use of colour. Green flash and sun yellow appear only in moments of significance, not as decorative wallpaper. The overall feel is confident, warm and human.
Buttons
Primary button: white background, #2E86AB ocean blue border and text, hover fills with #2E86AB and text turns white.
Secondary button / CTA: #00B4D8 cyan border and text on white background, hover fills cyan, text turns white.
Imagery Direction
Clean, natural, light-filled. Ocean, coastline, surf, wide open skies. People in natural environments — not staged corporate shots. Aerial or wide landscape shots work well as hero backgrounds. Always light and optimistic in mood. Golden hour light preferred. Never dark, moody or heavily filtered.
